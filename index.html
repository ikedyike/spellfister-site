About the Game

Chaotic spellcasting. Broken builds. One more run.

SpellFister is a solo-developed roguelite fantasy FPS focused on expressive spellcasting, fast movement, and arena-based combat.

Fight through replayable runs, survive projectile-heavy enemy waves, and experiment with upgrades that change how your magic works.
