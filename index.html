About

SpellFister is a solo-developed roguelite fantasy FPS focused on expressive spellcasting, fast movement, and chaotic arena combat. Enter replayable runs, survive enemy waves, and experiment with upgrades that change how your magic works.

Features

  • Fast-paced first-person magic combat
  • Roguelite upgrades and replayable runs
  • Arena shooter encounters with projectile-heavy chaos
  • Spell and modifier systems for build experimentation
  • Solo-developed in Unity/C# by Ikes Games
